# Claude Plugin Directory — Submission Fields

- **Link to plugin:** https://github.com/adriennedev/content-engine
- **Plugin homepage:** https://github.com/adriennedev/content-engine
- **Plugin name:** Content Engine
- **Plugin description:** A weekly personal-brand content pipeline that mines ideas from your notes and saved social posts, maintains an idea bank spreadsheet, drafts platform-ready posts in your voice from a voice profile you define, and generates on-brand visuals. It refuses to fabricate stories or metrics and never publishes anything — you review, edit, and post everything yourself.
- **Example use cases:**
  - Example 1: "Run the weekly content run" — Claude pulls the week's ideas from your notes app and saved posts, updates the idea bank, drafts 4 posts in your voice with creative, and emails you the review package.
  - Example 2: "Turn this idea into a LinkedIn post" — Claude drafts it per your voice profile and format playbook, recommends the best format, and specs the accompanying visual.
  - Example 3: "Build the carousel for draft 2" — Claude generates 6-10 branded slides in your design tool sized for LinkedIn and Instagram, and shares the editable design link.
